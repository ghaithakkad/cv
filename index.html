  • Dug into research across the board: market and user, qualitative and quantitative, behavioral and attitudinal. Focused on root causes, not just symptoms, and used those insights to shape features that actually moved product metrics.

  • Worked inside cross-functional product squads with PMs and engineers, co-owning discovery, prioritization, and delivery through dual-track Agile.

  • Ran continuous discovery workshops to validate ideas before engineering committed. Cut development rework by about 30%.
