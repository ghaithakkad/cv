Challenge

Design and develop an intuitive, user-friendly consumer app to showcase cinema showtimes and related information across multiple cinemas in the UAE. The app needed to offer easy navigation and seamless functionality to enhance the cinema-going experience for hundreds of thousands of users.

Solution

User Research & Persona Development

Conducted extensive research to understand user preferences and behavior, helping us design an app that addressed their needs for quick access to cinema showtimes, film trailers, ratings, and additional details.

UX/UI Design

Developed a clean, organized design that categorized cinemas by city and cinema name for easy navigation. Each listing displayed film trailers, descriptions, ratings, and screening times, ensuring users could quickly make informed decisions.

Search & Filter Functionality

Introduced an intuitive search function allowing users to quickly find showtimes based on preferences (cinema name, movie, city). Filtering options let users sort by time, rating, and more.

Sharing & Social Features

Enabled easy sharing of showtimes with friends and family, promoting social engagement and word-of-mouth marketing for cinema brands.

Mobile Optimization

Designed the app to be fully responsive, ensuring seamless functionality across phones and tablets of all screen sizes.
